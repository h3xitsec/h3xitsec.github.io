Michael‚Äôs password

To answer the task 1 we must get ‚ÄúMichael‚Äôs password‚Äù. From http://hamlet.thm/index.html. We know that Michael‚Äôs username is ghost and that he‚Äôs obsessed with Hamlet and the vocabulary used by Shakespeare.

We also know the password policy for WebAnno from the file we got from the FTP server. With that in hands, let‚Äôs build a custom wordlist to bruteforce Michael‚Äôs password.
